What is good design?

A good design encapsulates a message while maintaining a viewer's attention. It uses imagery that builds off of an idea and enhances it to a higher level. It uses balance and alignment to organize its points (if any) and simplicity to not be overwhelming. I believe that design plays a huge role in everyday life through marketing. This is why I believe a good design enhances a message. It elaborates upon what cannot be shown with only words, and provides a more well-rounded piece overall. A good design should also leave an impact on the viewer. It should stimulate a feeling of joy, intrigue, anger, or anything between. This allows a design to be more significant than just an image or media.
